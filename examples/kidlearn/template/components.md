# KidLearn WeChat Components

## 1. Lesson Label

- Type: rich text.
- Purpose: gives the article a friendly course-note identity.
- Use: before the title.
- Style: small blue pill with white text.
- Example: `KIDLEARN NOTE / 01`

## 2. Chunky Title Card

- Type: rich text.
- Purpose: opening identity and article promise.
- Style: warm yellow card, 20px radius, red title, blue label, chunky gray offset border.
- Example: "给孩子做 AI 学习产品，先别急着加游戏化"

## 3. Parent-Friendly Intro

- Type: rich text.
- Purpose: turns the article into plain-language advice.
- Style: large body text, one short paragraph, no jargon.
- Example: "一个好的学习体验，不是让孩子一直点按钮，而是让他知道下一步为什么值得做。"

## 4. Reward Insight Card

- Type: rich text.
- Purpose: one decisive takeaway.
- Style: white card, red left accent, soft border, rounded corners.
- Example: "奖励不是烟花，而是确认孩子刚刚做对了一件事。"

## 5. Learning Tip

- Type: rich text.
- Purpose: practical suggestion.
- Style: blue heading, pale blue background, pill label.
- Example: "把提示写成下一步，而不是正确答案。"

## 6. Gentle Warning

- Type: rich text.
- Purpose: flags design risks without sounding harsh.
- Style: warm yellow background, orange text label, soft border.
- Example: "不要用倒计时制造紧张感。"

## 7. Checklist Block

- Type: rich text.
- Purpose: closing action list.
- Style: white card, green check markers, generous row height.
- Example rows: "目标清楚", "反馈温和", "进度可见".

## 8. Activity Map

- Type: image-based.
- Purpose: shows a learning flow or lesson path.
- Style: simple rounded nodes, red/blue/yellow accents, no dense text.
- Use as PNG/JPG in WeChat.

## 9. Reward Poster

- Type: image-based.
- Purpose: cover or section visual.
- Style: large headline, stars/badges, 2-3 bright colors.

## 10. Closing CTA

- Type: rich text.
- Purpose: gentle next step.
- Style: red outline card or blue soft card.
- Example: "下次看一个学习产品时，先问：孩子能不能看懂自己为什么进步了？"
